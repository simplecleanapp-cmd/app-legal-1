# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-02-02

This Privacy Policy describes how **Personal Agent** (the "App") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the App.

## 1\. Who we are

* **App name:** Personal Agent
* **Package/application ID:** simplecleanapp\_cmd.personal\_agent\_app
* **Contact:** simplecleanapp@gmail.com

## 2\. What information the App collects

The App may collect the following information depending on which features you use:

### 2.1 Content you provide

* **Text messages** you type into the App.
* **Images** you choose to attach using the device image picker.
* **Audio recordings** you record using the microphone feature.

### 2.2 Device permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

* **Microphone (RECORD\_AUDIO):** to record audio messages.

If you deny this permission, the audio recording feature will not work.

### 2.3 Diagnostics (optional / standard)

During development and testing, the App (and the underlying platform/SDKs) may generate **crash logs** or **diagnostic logs** on your device.

## 3\. How the App uses information

The App uses the information described above to:

* Provide chat and messaging functionality.
* Record and play back audio messages.
* Allow you to pick and preview images.
* Improve stability and performance (e.g., debugging crashes during testing).

## 4\. Where your data is stored

At this stage of development:

* **Text, image, and audio content is stored locally on your device** as part of the app experience.
* The App **does not upload your recorded audio or selected images to a remote server** by default.

If future versions add cloud sync or server-based messaging, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features are enabled.

## 5\. Sharing of information

The App:

* **Does not sell** your personal information.
* **Does not share** your recorded audio or selected images with third parties.

However, standard platform components (e.g., Android OS, Google Play services) may process limited technical data as part of normal device operation.

## 6\. Data retention and deletion

* Content is retained **locally on your device** for as long as you keep it.
* You can remove the App to delete locally stored data (subject to the operating system’s behavior).

## 7\. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information within the App, but no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

## 8\. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 9\. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the **Effective date** at the top when changes are made.
