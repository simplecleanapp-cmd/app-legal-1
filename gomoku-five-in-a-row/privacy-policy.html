Privacy Policy

1) Overview

This app is designed to work offline and can optionally support local network (LAN) play on your local Wi-Fi network.

2) Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share personal information.

3) Information Stored on Your Device

The app may store game settings, preferences, and statistics locally on your device. This data does not leave your device.

4) Local Network (LAN) Play

If you use LAN play, the app uses your local network to discover and connect to nearby players and to exchange game events (such as moves) during a match.

LAN play communication stays within your local network and is not sent to our servers.

Purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your full payment information. Google may collect and process information required to complete the transaction, according to Google's policies.

6) Third-Party Services

Other than Google Play Billing (only when you make a purchase), the app does not use third-party services that collect personal information (such as analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash reporting).
